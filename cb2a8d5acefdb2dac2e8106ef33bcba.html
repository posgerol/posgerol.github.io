5 common mistakes when Dating in apps

Dating apps and sites have made finding your partner easier and more accessible. It is not necessary to leave the house to meet a kindred spirit. You do not need to go abroad if you want to communicate with foreigners.

But many are still unlucky. One by one, they install apps, try to get acquainted, fail, and are removed in complete frustration. After a while, everything repeats itself.

We tell you what mistakes you should pay attention to, and how to fix them fix.

1. Many applications and profiles on different sites

It is a mistake to think that the more questionnaires you have, the higher the chance of meeting your partner. You spend more time, get frustrated with online Dating faster, and delete your accounts. Leave one, maximum of two apps that you like. Delete the rest. Try to take online Dating more seriously, then everything will work out.

By the way, the RusDate app is great for serious Dating. Designed for Dating Russian-speaking users from all over the world. Questionnaires are passed moderation is done manually, which minimizes the appearance of fakes and bots. Yes, and the audience is decent. Try it and don't thank me. :)

2. Too many dates with different people

The more meetings you have, the more likely you are to find the right person. But it's not. Very quickly you will get tired of preparing for each new meeting, communicating with new acquaintances will not be interesting. Don't rush from one date to another. Choose the most interesting and worthy candidates in your opinion. Better give yourself more time to search.

3. Don't spend all your free time on applications

Yes, online Dating also takes time: view profiles, send likes, and start a conversation. But at some point, you start visiting more often. Check your Inbox, new candidates, and you can't stop sending a couple of likes. Of course, you get tired of it quickly, you stop living real life.

Our advice: spend 20-30 minutes online Dating a day when you have time and are in a good mood. If you are tired and angry, it is unlikely that you will be able to start a pleasant conversation and make the right decision.

4. Empty conversation

Sometimes the communication ended quickly, because there was nothing to discuss but the conversation itself. "How was your day?" "Where do you work?" "Beautiful eyes!" Don't waste your time on empty conversations: Hello to the Department. Ask unexpected but interesting questions:

What event in your life did you remember? What country would you go to if you had an unlimited budget?

Such questions reveal the interlocutor and they give rise to discussions.

5. A long correspondence

If you want serious Dating and meetings in real life, you do not need to communicate online for a month. If the interlocutor does not want to meet, but only talks, so far, the next one. You're just wasting your time on a conversation that probably won't translate into a real meeting. Meet and communicate with those who are ready to meet and continue their acquaintance in person.
