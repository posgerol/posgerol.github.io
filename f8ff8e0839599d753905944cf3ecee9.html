6 tips on how to quickly find a company for the New year

Until the New year is just a few days away, and you haven't decided on a company? Celebrating alone is not the most joyous prospect? We tell you how and where to find a company for the New year!

1. Website or the Dating app is the fastest way to find a company for the New year.

Install the rusdate Dating app. Fill out the form and make sure to specify the purpose of Dating: whether it is a joint celebration or the creation of a serious relationship. Don't lie about your age and Hobbies. Application it will offer candidates who best fit the search criteria. It will only remain to choose and start Dating!

2. Only real photo!

It would be a shame to spend new year's eve with a person whose photos differ significantly from reality, wouldn't it? Think of those who are just as much looking for a mate as you are. Add fresh photos of your own, without a ton of photoshop. You don't have much time to figure out who and what they really look like. After the desired date, you can delete the photos.

3. Prepare original greeting

It is important to stand out from the rest. You can use various options in the Dating app: highlight your profile with color and animation from photos, or add your profile in search results. Or you can write an original greeting in the form. Don't forget to mention that you want to spend a holiday together night.

4. Don't invite them to your home

Even if it is a holiday, it does not mean that you need to call a little familiar person to your home. Arrange to meet near the main tree in the center of the city. Romantic and quite safe. Further look at the circumstances. Don't forget to tell your friends where you are going and with whom.

5. Be friendly

Show kindness. Be welcoming and optimistic. No one wants to celebrate the New year in the company of a sad, sullen person. You can pay a compliment to a new acquaintance, but a sincere one. Flattery is always felt.

6. If you feel that acquaintance does not bring joy-end the conversation

If during the conversation, the other person is reluctant and blurry answers, does not want to talk about themselves, does not ask questions, and you feel that the conversation is not being started, do not waste time. End the conversation and meet someone else. An important indicator is monosyllabic responses and long pauses in the dialog. Someone who wants to get acquainted, does it here and now. Don't waste time on people who aren't particularly interested.

Remember that there is always a person who is lonely, just like you. Who would like to meet New year with a pleasant companion. In the New year, the most magical miracles happen. So what are you waiting for?
